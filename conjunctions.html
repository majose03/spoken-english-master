🔗 Connect Your Sentences

Conjunctions are the extremely important "glue" that joins short sentences into long, beautiful, fluent sentences.

🚀 Top Common Mistake (India)

Never use two opposing conjunctions combining the same sentence. For example:
Wrong: Although he is rich, but he is humble. (Double conjunctions error).
Right: Although he is rich, he is humble. OR He is rich, but he is humble.

Wrong: Because it was raining, so I stayed home.
Right: Because it was raining, I stayed home. OR It was raining, so I stayed home.
