💫 Modal Verbs

Supercharge your English by expressing Ability, Permission, Advice, and Obligation accurately.

⚠️ The Golden Rule of Modal Verbs

1. Always use the base form of the verb (V1) immediately after a modal. Never use "to".
Correct: I can swim. | Wrong: I can to swim.
2. Never add 's', 'es', or 'ed' to a modal verb for he/she/it subjects.
Correct: She must go. | Wrong: She musts go.
