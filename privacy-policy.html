Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are extremely important to us.

Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to SpokenMaster. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle the information related to your use of our completely frontend-based static website. Because SpokenMaster operates without a backend server database, our data collection practices are minimal and transparent.

1. Data Collection & Local Storage

SpokenMaster is a "zero-backend" web application. This means we do not possess a central database where user accounts, passwords, or personal studying data are stored.

Instead, we utilize your browser's Local Storage technology to save your progress directly onto your physical device. The data saved includes:

  • Your selected translation language (e.g., Tamil, Malayalam, Telugu).
  • Checkmarks and completion status for Daily Tasks.
  • Your quiz high scores.

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

2. Cookies, Analytics and Third-Party Advertisements

To keep SpokenMaster free forever, we participate in third-party advertising networks, specifically Google AdSense. These networks may use cookies and web beacons to collect non-personally identifiable information whenever you visit our website.

Google AdSense uses "DART cookies":

  • Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

3. Web Speech API (Microphone Access)

Our website utilizes the browser's native Web Speech API (specifically in the Speaking Tasks section) to evaluate your spoken English.

When you click the microphone button, your browser may ask for permission to access your microphone. The audio is processed locally by the browser's speech recognition engine (often powered by Google on Chrome, or Apple on Safari). We do not record, store, or transmit audio files to our servers.

4. Links to Other Sites

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those web sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those sites.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. By continuing to use the site after changes have been made, you accept the new Privacy Policy.

6. Contacting Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please email us directly at privacy@spokenmaster.com or use our Contact page.
