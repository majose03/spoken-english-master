💡 Pro Tips for Indian Learners

  • Differentiate between 'v' and 'w'. 'V' involves your teeth and lower lip. 'W' is rounding your lips.
  • Be careful with creating a strong 'r' sound. In British English, 'r' inside a word (car, water) is often silent.
  • English is a stress-timed language. Not all syllables have equal length or emphasize.

Minimal Pairs Training

Minimal pairs are two words that vary by a single sound. Practicing these will fix confusion between similar sounds (like P/B, V/W, S/SH).
